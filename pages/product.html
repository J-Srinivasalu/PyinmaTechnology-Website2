Product Details

B-Temp Trace

This device measures the human body temperature based on the infrared energy emitted by the body. It also comes with an attendance logging mechanism without any human intervention. It quickly gives the measurement results after pointing the target to the temperature sensor with an alert / report mechanism. Also, this can be connected with the network and the data can be remotely accessed. Application of areas is schools, colleges, hospitals, offices

Features
